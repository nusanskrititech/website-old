Get Involved with Us

We are the Indian Students' Association at Northeastern University, Boston.

Through Sanskriti, we want to value your contribution to making NU a more vibrant community for Indian students. Please join and enhance our network of illustrious alumni like yourselves and help us spread 'Indian-ness' here at NU.

Join US
  • You are welcome to attend as an audience member or as a participant
  • We are absolutely thrilled when our alumni start a business anywhere, and we are ready to support you in all your endeavors!
  • We would also love to host any sessions, workshops, or webinars by you!

    Please reach out to alumni@nusanskriti.org to express your interest in any event.

Be a part of the diverse Indian community with NU Sanskriti, and get a stage to display your creativity and talent!

Help Us

As you may know, funding for NUSanskriti is becoming increasingly difficult to come by. Despite this, we are committed to providing the best possible resources and support for our students. However, we need your help to make this a reality. Any contribution no matter how small would be greatly appreciated and would make a big difference in the lives of our students. We implore you to donate to us to empower the next generation of Indian students at our University!
