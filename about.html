Who We Are

NU Sanskriti is the Indian Students' Association at Northeastern University, Boston. We bring together Indian students and friends of Indian culture to celebrate our heritage, support each other, and create lifelong memories. Our community is open, vibrant, and always growing!

Our Mission

To create a home away from home for Indian students, promote cultural exchange, and provide academic, professional, and social support to our members. We strive to make every member feel welcome and empowered to succeed.

What We Do

  • Promote Indian culture and values on campus
  • Encourage leadership and personal growth
  • Foster a sense of belonging and community
  • Organize cultural festivals and celebrations (Diwali, Holi, and more)
  • Host networking and professional development events
  • Support new students with resources and mentorship
