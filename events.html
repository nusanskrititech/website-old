Past Events

Mohabbatein

A night of love and friendships, and a chance to ask that special someone to dance!

Aarambh

Hey newbies! let go of your jet-lags and join us for a special DJ night just for you!

Republic Day

Aye mere vatan ke logon, express your musical talents in tribute to India's republic day with a karaoke night!

Mehfil

The festivities had to wait till next year, but our singers surely couldn't! The last edition of Mehfil before the winter break was a wonderful conclusion to a spectacular year and has left us wanting for more!

NU Deepavali

Deepavali had us enthralled in beautiful twinkles and shimmering lights, and to add to the joyous atmosphere, the NU Deepavali night was just perfect, with an exciting lineup of music, dance and fashion, along with some mouth-watering food!

Rangeelo Raas

A much awaited night full of energy and joy, Rangeelo Raas made us swing to the wonderful rhythms of garba, and everyone's colourful ghagra cholis and kediyu adorned the venue!

Mehfil Aarambh

The melodious evening of Mehfil Aarambh was a great stage for the new musical talents of NU Sanskriti to shine, and left everyone wanting for more!

Onam Celebration

The fun-filled celebration of Onam- the harvest festival of Kerela, occurred on 18th September at Krentzman Quad, and was a gala of music, dance, and games, ending with delicious Payasam!

Ganesh Chaturthi

From 'Ganpati bappa morya!' to 'pudhcha varshi lavkar ya!' the 7 days of Ganesh utsav were a glorious celebration, filled with daily aartis and ofcourse, delightful modaks! The aartis conducted in different locations around campus, commenced on 31st August from Krentzmann squad and concluded with Ganesh Visarjan on 6th Sept at Centennial common.

Bhangra Workshop

The Bhangra workshops conducted in the honor of the Vaisakhi month left us panting, but they surely made everyone dance their worries away!

Tarang

An event that is best described as a night full of music, dance, and glamor! The amazing music dance and fashion teams put up enthralling performances for the audience in Blackman Auditorium.

Holi

With colors in the air and a groovy DJ, the celebration of Holi, the festival of colors occurred on 18th March 2022 at Centennial Common.
