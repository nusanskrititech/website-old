About Us

We are the Indian Students' Association at Northeastern University, Boston.

NU Sanskriti is one of the biggest Indian Students' Associations in the United States catering to over 7900 active Indian students at Northeastern University.

We aim to keep the traditions radiant and flourishing, bridge gaps between students of diverse backgrounds, cater them to meet fellow students and provide a common platform them to showcase their talents in various art forms.

  • Celebrating festivals and national days with 20+ events

    Feeling nostalgic for Indian festivals? Join us and take part in all the major events including Onam, Ganesh Chaturthi, Diwali, and much more!

  • 250+ participants across events

    Be a part of the diverse Indian community with NU Sanskriti, and get a stage to display your creativity and talent!
